## INSTRUCTION 3 OF 5: T1.8 — Sales Conversations Mastery (2-Hour Training)

Create the COMPLETE discovery call and sales conversation training.

**Reference Page 17: https://www.notion.so/398fafae51be818fa910ee2d203b681e**

**Part 1: Discovery Call Framework (30 min)** — 4-segment structure:
1. Rapport + Context (5min) — "Before we dive in, I want to make sure this is useful for you."
2. Discovery (15min) — 7 core questions:
   - "What's the most expensive leadership decision your org is facing?"
   - "How are you currently making that decision? What data do you have?"
   - "If you could measure one thing about your leadership org with perfect accuracy, what would it be?"
   - "Have you used leadership diagnostics before?"
   - "What would a successful outcome from this conversation look like?"
   - "Who else needs to be involved in any decision?"
   - "What's the timeline pressure?"
3. Frame (5min) — Connect to Three Forces. "Based on what you've shared..."
4. Next Steps (5min) — Clear CTA.

Diagnostic trigger identification: which prospect statements → which diagnostic recommendation.

**Part 2: ICP Mapper Usage (20 min)** — How to use LIVE during calls. Practice exercise: given persona + pain point, find mapper entry and craft conversation starter. (Cross-ref CD-2 Instruction 3.)

**Part 3: Objection Role-Plays (40 min)** — 15 objections with structured role-play. For each: coach plays prospect, trainee uses 5-part response framework. Key: "Never defensive. Always curious. Data not opinions." (Cross-ref CD-2 Instruction 1.)

**Part 4: Close & Handoff (30 min)** — 3-option close (diagnostic-only / diagnostic+advisory / diagnostic+programme). Handoff from sales to delivery. Follow-up email template (within 24hr). CRM documentation requirements.
